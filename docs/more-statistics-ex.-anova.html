Chapter 7 More Statistics (ex. Anova)

The reader may wonder what the asterisks indicate in Figure 3E. These denote statistically significant difference between the AUC from each day and 0. This can be computed using one-sample t-test.

The reader may be curious as to how she can determine whether the AUCs across five days significantly differ. If the AUCs significantly differ, then the results might be more interesting. This can be achieved by using one-way analysis of variance (ANOVA).
